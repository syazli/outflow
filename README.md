# Outflow

**Outflow** is a minimalist and intuitive expense tracker.

---

## Features

*   **Local Storage:** All your financial data is stored securely on your device.
*   **Transaction Management:** Seamlessly **Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD)** your financial records.
*   **Income & Expense Tracking:** Categorize every cent by:
    *   Name
    *   Category
    *   Amount
    *   Date
*   **Real-time Calculations:**
    *   **Total Balance:** Instant calculation of `Income - Expense`.
    *   **Cumulative Totals:** View your total earnings and total spending at a glance.

---

## Tech Stack

*   **Language:** Kotlin
*   **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose
*   **Architecture:** MVVM
*   **Animations:** Lottie 
*   **Local Database:** Room Database
